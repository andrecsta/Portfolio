Enhancing Customer Experience and Boosting Sales in E-commerce

Machine Learning - 2023

This project revolves around an e-commerce company that uses two sales channels: a website and a mobile app. To facilitate purchases, customers are required to create personalized accounts and log in, enabling them to access a wide range of products and services. In an effort to optimize user engagement and satisfaction, the system diligently records the duration of each login session, tracking user activity across both the app and website platforms.

Due to budgetary constraints, the decision-makers must focus on either optimizing the website or refining the app to achieve the desired results: Implement targeted improvements tailored to the chosen platform, seeking to create an immersive and effortless shopping experience that captivates and retains customers.

Know-how

Through a data-driven approach, this project starts with the overall analysis of the datasets. Then, as we understood our data, we chose the relevant metrics and applied three Linear Regression models.

We chose the RMSE metric to evaluate them.

  • Regular Linear Regression: RMSE: 2.786112
  • Ridge Regression: RMSE: 2.786423
  • Lasso Regression: RMSE: 2.790789

Having values too close to each other, we chose the Regular Linear Regression model as the best one.

Conclusion

Our goal is to predict the target variable valor_total_gasto (total amount spent). Interpreting the coefficients, they tell us the following:

  • A 1 unit increase in Tempo de Cadastro (Registration Time) is associated with an increase of R$63.74 in the client's valor_total_gasto per month.
  • A 1 unit increase in Número Médio de Cliques por Sessão (Average Number of Clicks per Session) is associated with an increase of R$26.23 in the client's valor_total_gasto per month.
  • A 1 unit increase in Tempo Logado no App (Time Logged in the App) is associated with an increase of R$38.57 in the client's valor_total_gasto per month.
  • A 1 unit increase in Tempo Logado no Website (Time Logged on the Website) is associated with an increase of R$0.68 in the client's valor_total_gasto per month.

Is it worthwhile to invest in the website? No, the gain is minimal.

Is it worthwhile to invest in the app? Yes, for every 1 unit increase in the time logged in, there can be an increase of R$38.57.

Nevertheless, increasing customer retention (measured by the variable Tempo de Cadastro or Registration Time) proves to be the most profitable action.
